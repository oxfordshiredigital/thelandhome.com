MEET YOUR TEAM

The people behind our friendly, efficient service.


Andrew's family business has over sixty years experience in obtaining planning permissions, for both its own sites and for other landowners. Mark has been promoting sites for over twenty-five years, engaging with local communities and Local and District Councils to achieve planning.

Andrew Jewson

Director
Andrew's family property business, SGJ Ltd, was established in 1955. Andrew joined the company in 1986 as Finance Manager. He became involved with the property development team, acquiring sites and dealing with the planning process. For the last five years he has been helping other landowners to promote their sites and gain planning permissions.

Mark Pritchett

Director
Mark's early career was in business development for a boutique finance house, where he procured and oversaw a £500 million asset portfolio. Clients included Shimizu Corporation, one of the largest construction companies in the world. For the past 25 years Mark has been acquiring, promoting and developing land. He recently achieved planning consent for housing on a green-belt site located on the outskirts of a Grade 2 listed village.

Martyn Ellis

Operations
Martyn is responsible for our Site Assessment and Management. An Honours Graduate of Manchester University, with a Masters Degree in Analytical Science, he has been the Operations Manager for SGJ Ltd for the past 12 years and now runs our on-site and office services.



Neil Pearce

Avon Planning Services
Neil holds a Bachelor of Arts Honours degree in Urban and Regional Planning and a Diploma in Town Planning. He is a Chartered Town Planner and member of the Royal Town Planning Institute (RTPI), and a former Director and Trustee of the Town and Country Planning Summer School Charity. With over 16 years of experience in local government, having worked for Bournemouth Borough Council, Coventry City Council, Stratford-on-Avon District Council and as a Development Manager at Wychavon District Council, he set up his own independent consultancy in 2003. APS assists a broad range of clients including local planning authorities, town and parish councils, architectural practices, private developers and land owners.

Jayne Cashmore

Oakwood Planning
Jayne is a member of the Royal Town Planning Institute. She currently runs her own private planning consultancy, Oakwood Planning, advising clients in relation to a variety of different developments including residential, commercial and rural planning matters. Jayne holds a First Class Joint Honours degree in Geography and Town Planning and a Masters with Distinction in Spatial Planning. She has worked for County, District and City Council’s at all levels from assistant planner through to senior planner and management roles. For the last 4 years of her Local Authority career Jayne managed the Planners, Forestry & Landscape Team at Stratford on Avon District Council. She has a good understanding of the planning process from local authority and private perspectives, having worked for 13 years in Local Government and also carries out her own small scale renovation/property development projects.


We work with preferred, specialised consultants to help a landowner achieve the best planning for their land. Planning applications involve consultants from many disciplines: Architecture, Highways, Master Planning, Archaeological, Landscaping, Ecology, Topology, Flooding, Soil, Contamination, Arboriculture, Legal Services and Accountants.
