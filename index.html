How it works . . .


We pay for and manage the entire planning process. Only if planning is obtained do we share in any profit after a successful sale - otherwise we foot all the bills.

We also buy land whilst still allowing you to benefit from any future planning potential.

The planning process is becoming ever more complex and it takes time, experience, money and enthusiasm to navigate. At The Land Home we have the skills and resources to help achieve your planning goals.

We handle and pay for the entire Planning Process. This includes the Promotion of the site until Planning has been achieved, including working within Neighbourhood and Local Development Frameworks.

We promote land nationwide, with particular focus on Central and Southern England, and the Home Counties, where land is most in demand.


We would like to hear from you if you are:

  • The Owner of land which you believe has potential for housing.
  • The Owner of land identified in your local neighbourhood development plan as having possible residential potential.
  • The Owner of an existing business or industry close to residential areas, and are looking to relocate or sell.
  • The Owner of a potential residential site frustrated with the progress or value of your current deal.
  • The Owner of an option on land with residential potential.

WHAT DOES LAND PROMOTION MEAN FOR LANDOWNERS?

As a landowner, you only get to sell your land once.

It is vital, therefore, that we help you achieve maximum value, but we also need to identify the most suitable planning mix and development in order to achieve Planning Permission for you. Our knowledge makes the Planning, Promotion and Application process transparent and straightforward.

We understand that every Land Promotion challenge is unique and that every landowner has different circumstances.

Very often there are joint owners or families involved and good communication is the key. That is why we pride ourselves on working in partnership with you and being sensitive to each landowner's individual needs.

We are happy to help, whether you own one acre or one hundred.
