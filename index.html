How it works . . .


Our expertise helps Landowners to maximise the value of their land by obtaining the most suitable Planning Permission. We then help to sell the land for the best price to Developers and Builders.

The Planning Permission process is becoming ever more complex and it takes time, experience, money and enthusiasm to navigate. At The Land Home we have the skills and resources to help you achieve your planning goals.

We handle and pay for the entire Planning Process. This includes the Promotion of the site until Planning has been achieved, including working within Neighbourhood & Local Development Frameworks.

We are based in Oxfordshire, but we promote land nationwide, with particular focus on Central and Southern England, and the Home Counties, where land is most in demand.

WHAT DOES LAND PROMOTION MEAN FOR LANDOWNERS?

As a Landowner, you only get to sell your land once.

It is therefore vital that we help you achieve maximum value, but we also need to identify the most suitable planning mix and development in order to achieve Planning Permission for you. Our knowledge makes the Planning, Promotion and Application process transparent and straightforward.

We understand that every Land Promotion challenge is unique and that every Landowner has different circumstances.

Very often there are joint owners or families involved and good communication is the key. That is why we pride ourselves on working in partnership with you and being sensitive to each Landowner's individual needs.

We are happy to help, whether you own one acre or one hundred.
